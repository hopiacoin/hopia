Our Story

HOPIA is the story of a lonely frog and a forgotten dog who were left behind by the world. Together they found hope, friendship, and a caring community. HOPIA represents resilience, unity, love, and the belief that no one should be forgotten.

Roadmap

Phase 1 - Building Hope

Launch X and Telegram, build the story, reach the first 100 members, attract contributors and increase community activity.

Phase 2 - Growing Together

Expand the community, build a volunteer team, improve engagement, host events and grow social media presence.

Phase 3 - Community First

Strengthen the brand, build partnerships, and prepare the foundation and tokenomics for the future.

Phase 4 - Together We Rise Again

Official token launch, reward major contributors, and continue expanding the HOPIA ecosystem.
